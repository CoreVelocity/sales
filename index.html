Wi-Fi & network — important notes

XS1: operates best on an 802.11ac (5 GHz, WPA2) Wi-Fi network with a minimum of 15 Mbps download and 5 Mbps upload per unit's network.
XP1: requires wired Ethernet connectivity (Cat6 cable) — plan a network drop at each XP1 location.
